B0x Token

Proof of Work Mining & Liquidity Staking Protocol

Executive Summary

B0x Token represents the next evolution in proof-of-work mining, designed as a Version 2 upgrade to the original 0xBitcoin protocol. Built on Ethereum as an ERC20 token, B0x addresses the mining halt experienced by 0xBitcoin while introducing innovative economic mechanisms that balance miner incentives with protocol sustainability.

Key Innovation

B0x introduces a revolutionary fee redistribution system where 50% goes to miners and 50% to liquidity providers, creating a sustainable economic model that prevents overmining while ensuring long-term protocol viability.

Protocol Overview

31.2M Total Supply
21M Max Mined Tokens (0xBitcoin + B0x)
10.2M Max Staking Rewards
1x-4x Difficulty Adjustment Range
10 min Target Block Time

B0x Token serves as an optional upgrade path for 0xBitcoin holders, designed to complete the remaining 100+ years of mining that was halted due to a contract bug. The protocol maintains full reversibility through the RightsTo0xBitcoin token mechanism, allowing users to return to the original 0xBitcoin at any time.

Technical Improvements

Dynamic Difficulty Adjustment

4x up and 4x down difficulty adjustments with emergency protocols for periods exceeding 7-14 days, ensuring network stability. This is because we use 2016 blocks like Bitcoin.

Challenge Reuse System

Challenges are reused 2016 times before renewal, enabling multiple miners to submit simultaneously and improving network participation.

Layer 2 Integration

21.5M tokens bridged to Base Layer 2 for efficient staking and minting, while maintaining mainnet security for core operations.

Economic Model

Fee Structure & Distribution

  • Mining Fee Distribution 50% Miners / 50% LPs
  • Variable Mining Fee Based on Difficulty
  • Demand Fee Multiplier Up to 16x for Fast Mining
  • Time Bonus Maximum 200 Minutes Optimal
  • Maximum Contract Depletion Rate Infinity!
  • Minimum Contract Depletion Rate 50% in 1-2 Years

B0x Token operates on a 31.2M total supply model with strategic allocation: 21M tokens dedicated to proof-of-work mining and 10.2M allocated to staking rewards. The protocol implements sophisticated difficulty adjustment mechanisms with 2016 blocks per standard adjustment period. Emergency adjustments activate under specific conditions: 7 days if the network reaches 4x target blocktime with at least 256 blocks mined, or 14 days at target Time of 10 minute epochs. The halving schedule operates on 210,000 epochts per halvening @ 10 minute average block time similar to Bitcoin, ensuring sustainable and long-term emission.

Mining & Hardware Support

Multi-Platform Mining Support

CPU • GPU (NVIDIA/AMD) • FPGA Compatible

Windows & Ubuntu Mining Software Available

B0x Token launches at difficulty 1, making it accessible to CPU and GPU miners from day one. The community-driven mining ecosystem includes public software for all major platforms, ensuring broad participation and decentralization. As network adoption grows, difficulty scales naturally based on miner participation.

Liquidity Staking Protocol

Uniswap V4 Integration

Custom Hook contract enables dynamic swap fee adjustments to optimize returns for staking participants.

NFT-Based Staking

Accepts custom Uniswap V4 Pool NFTs created through the official dApp interface for enhanced security.

Dual Token Liquidity

B0x Token paired with 0xBitcoin creates a unique liquidity pool connecting both ecosystems.

Revenue Sharing

50% of minted B0x tokens and 50% of the mining fees are distributed to liquidity providers over time. Also the contract claims any fees gained from holding the Uniswap NFTs to redistribute 100% back to stakers.

Withdrawal Penalty

There is a withdrawl penalty ranging from 20% instant to ~0% after 45-90 days. It goes down quickly to 15 days for 3% penalty.

Risk Considerations

As with any experimental protocol, B0x Token carries inherent risks including smart contract vulnerabilities, market volatility, and regulatory uncertainty. The optional upgrade mechanism provides flexibility but requires careful management of multiple token positions. Mining profitability varies based on network conditions and hardware efficiency.

Getting Started

Join the B0x Token ecosystem at launch for optimal mining opportunities. Early participants benefit from low initial difficulty and growing protocol value. Access mining software, staking interfaces, and community resources through the official dApp.
